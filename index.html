Berserk

Berserk is a dark and epic manga that follows the story of Guts, a lone mercenary warrior seeking vengeance against his former best friend who sacrificed their comrades in arms to become a god-like ruler. Along the way, he battles hordes of demonic creatures and confronts his own inner demons in a world ruled by darkness and chaos.

Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a gripping manga that takes place during the Viking Age and follows the story of Thorfinn, a young warrior seeking revenge against the man who killed his father. As he travels with a band of mercenaries, Thorfinn confronts the brutality of war and the futility of revenge, ultimately finding himself drawn to a peaceful way of life in a land known as Vinland. With its complex characters, intense action scenes, and exploration of themes like honor, morality, and the nature of violence, Vinland Saga is a manga that will leave readers thinking long after the final page.

One Piece

One Piece is a captivating manga that follows the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with a rubber body and an unwavering dream of becoming the Pirate King. Along with his diverse and quirky crew of allies, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy embarks on an epic adventure filled with thrilling battles, unexpected alliances, and unforgettable characters. In a world of treasure, magic, and powerful foes, Luffy and his crew must overcome countless challenges and overcome their own personal demons to achieve their ultimate goal.

Mob Pyscho 100

Mob Psycho 100 is an intriguing manga that follows the story of Shigeo Kageyama, a young boy with incredibly powerful psychic abilities who struggles to fit in with his peers. As he works as an assistant to a fraudulent psychic, Mob encounters various supernatural phenomena and learns to control his powers, all while navigating the complex and often emotional landscape of adolescence. With its vibrant artwork and exploration of themes like self-acceptance, personal growth, and the blurred lines between reality and imagination, Mob Psycho 100 is a manga that appeals to readers of all ages.

Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man is a thrilling and gory manga that follows the story of Denji, a young man who merges with a chainsaw demon to become a powerful and ruthless devil hunter. With a diverse and eccentric cast of characters, Chainsaw Man explores themes of identity, morality, and the nature of power as Denji and his allies battle hordes of deadly devils in a world where humans can make deals with the supernatural. With its unique blend of horror, action, and dark humor, Chainsaw Man is a manga that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.
